About - Project

Expression_(Image Classification) created using MobileNet Built with p5.js.

Train a Neural Network to distinguish between your Happy Face or Sad Face)

How To Use

In this model, you can classify anything... So here I classify between Happy Expression and Sad Expression.

Steps you need to follow (For Classify between Happy and Sad Expression)

  • Add your Happy Expression photo by clicking ADD HAPPY IMAGES button as many as you want. Do the same for ADD SAD IMAGES.

  • Train Model -> Once you happy with your data (happy/sad Images) it is time to Train Model. Just Simply click on TRAIN MODEL and wait some seconds. After when training is complete you'll get a total loss.

It is time to check your model

  • Really Simple. Just press START GUESSING and see the output with label and confidence.

follow the same procedure for all type of classification.
